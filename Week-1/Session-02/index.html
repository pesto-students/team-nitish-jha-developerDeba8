Guwahati, Assam

Location

Guwahati is Assam's largest Indian district, and also the largest metropolis in northeastern India. It is located on the southern bank of the Brahmaputra, a major riverine port city along with hills.

About

Weather

The average annual temperature in Guwahati is 24.0 °C | 75.3 °F. The annual rainfall is 3038 mm | 119.6 inch. Guwahati are in the northern hemisphere. Summer begins at the end of June and ends in September.

Population

The current metro area population of Gauhati in 2023 is 1,176,000, a 1.82% increase from 2022. The metro area population of Gauhati in 2022 was 1,155,000, a 1.76% increase from 2021
